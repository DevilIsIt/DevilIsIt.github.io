Precautions

  • What you should and shouldn't do during and after landslide

  • DOs


    1. Stay Alert:Listen to weather updates and warnings from local authorities.
    2. Evacuate Immediately:Move to higher ground or a safe area.Follow evacuation routes provided by authorities
    3. Protect Yourself:If caught indoors, take cover under a sturdy table or piece of furniture.If outdoors, move uphill or away from the landslide path.
